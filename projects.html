My Projects

I am currently in the process of building a keyboard. I ordered a pcb, microprocessor, resistors, diodes, a case, etc, from winkeyless.kr, and I will be soldering the pieces together myself and programming the micrprocessor when it is finished. The switches are Cherry MX Clears and the keycaps are DSA blanks.

I have also built a few computers. My own computer I built in January 2015, I built my little brother a PC this past November, and I have helped a few roommates in the building of their own computers. Because of this, I've got a much better understanding of how important drivers are for things to work, at least in Windows.
